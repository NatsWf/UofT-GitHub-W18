About Me

I’m a Visual Designer who has navigated between the worlds of Print and Digital Design for many years. I'm detailed oriented and I'm constantly looking for new challenges to improve my work. Working on different kinds of projects has given me the confidence to explore new ideas while collaborating in a team where I can learn from different roles.

I’m crazy about knitting, ramen, and cats. But, above all, I love Design in all its forms.

If you believe I could be a good fit for your project, don’t hesitate to send me a “Hello!” using the email below. I’d be more than happy to discuss the opportunity of working together with you!
