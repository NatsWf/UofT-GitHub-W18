Plan My Trip

A travel app for people
who don’t love planning

About The Project

This project was a design challenge where I was supposed to outline a product based on current pains travelers have. On my interviews, I found that most people dislike planning. This app was my solution.

The Problem: Users were overwhelmed by the amount of work and information planning a trip takes.

The Solution: Creating an app the holds all the information necessary for planning, like hotels, attractions based on the user's budget and preference.

My Role: UX/UI Designer and Researcher

Tools: Figma, Photoshop

Personal Objective: My goal was to create a flow that was enjoyable and easy for the user.
